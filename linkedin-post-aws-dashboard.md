**Alternative Professional Version:**

Sharing insights from a recent project: building a real-time AWS service discovery platform entirely through AI-assisted development.

**The Challenge**
Engineering teams designing AWS solutions need quick answers: "Is service X available in region Y?" Manual documentation checks are time-consuming and error-prone.

**The Approach**
Leveraged serverless architecture (Lambda, EventBridge, S3) with modular design across four independent repositories. Each component had a focused responsibility, making AI-assisted development manageable and effective.

**Key Takeaway**
Strategic planning matters more than ever with AI tools. Spending time on architecture and clear component boundaries enabled smooth development sessions and resulted in a maintainable, production-ready system.
